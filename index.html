The Growth of Tourism

Earlier our ancestors used to travel by sea routes as it was a convenient and most affordable medium but it was time taking.Dues technological advancement we can now easily travel to any place without wasting time we can travel thousands of miles within a few hours. Technological advancement has shrunk the earth into a global village. Besides, the modern modes are much safer than the modes that our predecessors used.

Effect of Tourism on a Country

For any country, tourism generates a lot of money especially a country like India. Due to the Taj Mahal (one of the seven wonders of the world) every year the government raise a huge sum of revenue. Also, because of tourism other industries also bloom. Such industries include transportation, wildlife, arts and entertainment, accommodation, etc.

Moreover, this ultimately leads to the creation of job and other opportunities in the area. But there are some drawbacks too which can affect the lifestyle and cultural value of the country.

Benefits of Tourism

It is give information to tourists in various parts of knowledge especially about different cultures. They know important part of cultures such as dances, instruments, foods and drinks, products and so on. They learn how to dance with specific movement and special clothes. For example, plate dance from Minangkabau culture in West Sumatera, Indonesia which take two plates on two hands when dancer is dancing and they cannot be fallen down. In the end of dancing, they must step on piece of glasses. Then, they can try to play local instruments and produce music by it, such as angklung instrument. They can eat many traditional food, see the process how to cook that food and try to cook, such as beef rendang from West Sumatera, Aceh noodle and rembige satay from Lombok. They also can see special souvenir and buy it for their family and friends.

Benefits Due to Tourism

As we know that tourism contributes a lot to the revenue of the country. Also, the government uses this income for the growth and development of the country. Likewise, they construct dams, wildlife sanctuaries, national parks, Dharamshala and many more.
In conclusion, we can say that tourism is a very productive activity both for the tourist and the government. As they support each other simultaneously. Also, the government should consider improving the conditions of the country as more and more number of tourist visit their country.
